From the President's Desk

 
                                                            --Rituraj Borgohain

At the foremost, I would like to mention that it is indeed a great privilege for me to hold the position of the President of AEC chess club 2018 - 21. I began my tenure with the perspective that I had to create and maintain an environment where students could freely display their talents in the field of chess. So keeping that positive attitude in mind, AEC Chess club provides the platform for individuals to grab the opportunities to take part in various events and competitions and thus shape their careers. I am glad to inform that this is the first time we are working on a website of ours. This marks the continuity of the glorious history of the club and creative talents of the premier institution. I am hopeful that the present core-commitee members will contribute to stimulate the chess audience and keep up the rich heritage of the college. I would like to convey my heartiest wishes to all the members of AEC Chess Club.

The pin is mightier than the sword.

Fred Reinfeld
