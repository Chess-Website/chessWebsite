All India Open Rapid Rating Chess Championship, 2019

From the President's Desk

 
                                                            --Rituraj Borgohain

At the foremost, I would like to mention that it is indeed a great privilege for me to hold the position of the President of AEC chess club 2018 - 21. I began my tenure with the perspective that I had to create and maintain an environment where students could freely display their talents in the field of chess. So keeping that positive attitude in mind, AEC Chess club provides the platform for individuals to grab the opportunities to take part in various events and competitions and thus shape their careers. I am glad to inform that this is the first time we are working on a website of ours. This marks the continuity of the glorious history of the club and creative talents of the premier institution. I am hopeful that the present core-commitee members will contribute to stimulate the chess audience and keep up the rich heritage of the college. I would like to convey my heartiest wishes to all the members of AEC Chess Club.

All India Open Rapid Rating Chess Championship


“I don’t believe in psychology. I believe in good moves.” – Bobby Fischer
True That , Good moves are the only way to succeed in this struggle of life , with a positive and fair mindset to tackle the difficulties and to compete in the gaming form , i.e Chess , The All India Open Rapid Rating Chess Championship 2019 , Held under AEC Chess Club , on 13th October , 2019 at the Room no.6 & 8 (Main Building) from morning 8:00 AM.
With over 253 participants including , 2 International Masters , 160 International Rated Chess Players the competition Was held under the Inauguration by our Principal Sir , the competition concluded with a total count of 61 winners , varying in various Age category and special prizes with additional cash prize worth of Rs. 1,11,111.
The championship title was bagged by IM Dhulipalla Bala Chandra Prasad , hailing from Andhra Pradesh.
Moreover , with an immense participation of over 10 AECIANS 5 of them managed to secure various category prizes.
Signing out with the hope of further enthusiasm ,
Long_Live_AEC

Greetings Everyone !!!
The 4th Shatranj Open Prize Money Lichess Championship 2021 was successfully organized by the AEC CHESS CLUB on 22nd May, 2021. The event went on smoothly and we present our vote of thanks to every participant for giving their time and support. This time, we got many players from Assam as well as from outside the state. A Fide Master (FM) ranked player also participated in the event !
We would like to give special credits to Mr. Ravikant Tiwari ( Chief Arbiter) and Mr. Ashok Baruah (Teacher-in-charge, AEC CHESS CLUB) for their unconditional support and help in organizing this tournament.
A total of 141 players participated in the 4th Shatranj Open Prize Money Lichess Championship 2021, which is the highest number of participants in any of the Shatranj editions !
